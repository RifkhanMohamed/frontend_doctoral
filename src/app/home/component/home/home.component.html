The main objective of the Center for Doctoral Studies at University Mohammed V CEDoc is to train doctoral students in academic research through a path of excellence and innovation. The multidisciplinary approach creates a center of expertise between the university's different research programs to respond to the issues and challenges of Morocco and Africa. The CEDoc aims to support doctoral students in the different stages of preparing their thesis by providing them with specific and complementary training to academic standards of research and excellence.

The Center for Doctoral Studies at University Mohammed V attracts the attention of students holding a master's degree, an engineering degree or equivalent.

Hold a master's degree, an engineering diploma or a recognized equivalent diploma and meet the admission criteria specified in the descriptions of doctoral training, in particular the adequacy between the research subject and the diplomas.

For a complete application, we ask applicants to submit:

• A CV of 2 pages maximum

• Research publications or patents, if applicable

• Research publications or patents, if applicable

• A cover letter indicating relevant extracurricular activities, if applicable

• Academic files: certified copies of diplomas (with detailed transcripts)

• Certified copy of identity card and/or passport.

• 2 letters of recommendation

• The research project proposal (2 – 3 pages): Writing a research proposal (2 to 3 pages) on the subject specifying its scope, the questions asked, the research hypotheses, the theoretical field of investigation and conceptual, the potential benefits for the benefit of the UM6P, Morocco or Africa.

The review committee will verify that the proposed project matches the applicant's background and experience and then rank the applications based on excellence to invite the highest ranked applicants for an interview (oral presentation and/or written test).

The UM6P CEDoc communicates the decision of the admissions committee to the candidates in writing.
